## Идея 1: Industrial Data Loft

**Design Movement**: Neo-Industrial / Data Brutalism
**Core Principles**: Raw data transparency, functional density, industrial warmth

## Идея 2: Scandinavian Data Canvas

**Design Movement**: Nordic Minimalism meets Data Visualization Art
**Core Principles**: Clarity through restraint, purposeful color, breathing space, data as art
**Color Philosophy**: Warm white (#FAFAF8) canvas with deep navy (#0F172A) text. Teal (#0D9488) as primary action color — professional yet distinctive. Soft amber (#F59E0B) for high-priority highlights.
**Layout Paradigm**: Card-based mosaic layout for company profiles. Left-aligned content hierarchy. Wide margins with intentional negative space. Horizontal scrolling data strips for city comparisons.
**Signature Elements**: Rounded pill-shaped tags for segments, soft shadow cards with hover lift, donut charts with center labels for city distribution.
**Interaction Philosophy**: Cards expand on click with smooth morph transition. Filters use toggle pills instead of dropdowns. Search with instant highlight matching.
**Animation**: Fade-in-up on scroll for sections, elastic spring on card hover, smooth number counting on stat cards, subtle pulse on high-priority items.
**Typography System**: "Plus Jakarta Sans" for headings (warm, friendly geometric), "Source Sans 3" for body, tabular numbers for data alignment.

## Идея 3: Executive Command Center

**Design Movement**: Corporate Intelligence Dashboard / Bloomberg Terminal Aesthetic
**Core Principles**: Information density without clutter, executive decision-making focus, real-time feel, authority through precision
**Color Philosophy**: Near-black (#0C0C14) background with electric blue (#3B82F6) data highlights. Emerald (#10B981) for high potential, amber (#F59E0B) for medium, rose (#F43F5E) for low. White (#F8FAFC) text for maximum contrast.
**Layout Paradigm**: Multi-panel dashboard with resizable sections. Top command bar with global search and filters. Left panel for navigation/filters, center for main data grid, right for detail preview.
**Signature Elements**: Glowing borders on active elements, terminal-style data grids, heat-map coloring on potential scores, sparkline mini-charts in table cells.
**Interaction Philosophy**: Keyboard-first navigation, instant search with fuzzy matching, click-to-drill-down on any metric, side panel slides for company details.
**Animation**: Data pulse effect on load, smooth panel resize, typewriter effect on search, gradient sweep on hover rows.
**Typography System**: "Space Grotesk" for headings (tech-forward), "IBM Plex Mono" for data, "Inter" for UI labels.
